# Section Building Rules:

1. Township, range, and meridian: Township increases north, range increases west.

2. Sections: Numbered 1-36 in a serpentine pattern; section 1 is southeast.

3. Default offsets: 20.11 meters for surveyed boundaries, 30.16 meters for standard road allowances.

4. If scaling applies later, adjust to 20.12 and 30.18.

5. Correction lines: If total width is 25 or more, treat as unsurveyed; otherwise surveyed.

6. Correction lines appear every four townships (e.g., 58, 54), offset 5.03 meters inward.

7. 0/20.12 Section Line Intersection rule: Lines form continuous cross shapes-they meet seamlessly.

8. Alternating road allowances: Alternate with blind lines between sections.

9. L-shaped 30.16 lines: Extend down two sections, never crossing section lines-only quarter lines.

10. When a surveyed and unsurveyed road allowance meet, align the 20.11 lines; the 30.16 line stops at the 20.11 boundary.

11. Partial townships: Handle by adjusting or skipping boundaries.

12. 1/4 Lines must end on L-SEC / L-USEC (L-USEC-2012 or L-USEC-0) Lines

13. LSD Lines must end on Hard Section Boundaries (Not 30.18 Lines)

14. A Vertical Road Allowance Belong to the 1/4 to its right

15. A Horizontal Road Allowance Belong to the 1/4 above it

16. Blind Lines must end on L-SEC / L-USEC (L-USEC-2012 or L-USEC-0) Lines

17. The Bottom road allowance across Township 57/ the Top road allowance across Township is 56 is a "base Line" , every 4 Townships is a base line, so 52, 48, 44 etc.. this road allowance should be L-SEC By default. (this is always a priority over any other layer rules regarding these specific road allowances)

18. LSD Lines never end on a 30.18 Road Allowance Line

19. No line in the section building should fall within a 20.12 road allowance (surveyed or unsurveyed)
